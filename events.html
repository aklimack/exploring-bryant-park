Bryant Park Events

Bryant Park offers a variety of events and activities. Below is a list of some of the events that Bryant Park offers!

    Activities

  • JUGGLING- Free Juggling Lessons
  • FENCING- Free Beginner Lessons
  • BOOT CAMP- Rotation of Exercises

    Markets

  • FRENCH MARKET
  • Fresh New Jersey produce, artisan breads, cut flowers, cheeses, olive oils and more available for purchase. Operated by Bensidoun, the world's premiere operator of French Style markets. Rain or shine.
  • WINTER VILLAGE HOLIDAY SHOPS
  • The Holiday Shops feature food and artisanal boutiques offering exquisite gifts from distinctive apparel to jewelry, decorative goods, local foods, and much more. There's something for everyone!

    Summer Movie Showings

  • Classic films under the stars on the big screen. Bring a blanket and sit on the lawn to enjoy great food, friends, plus a classic Warner Brothers cartoon before each film.

    Ice Skating

  • WINTER ICE SKATING
  • EXERSKATE- Work up a sweat on the Rink with an on-ice circuit workout. Expect exercises used by figure and hockey skaters that will make you a better skater.
